Common Questions

Which product should I start with? +

For most people, Immunity Boost is the ideal starting point. If you've recently taken antibiotics or have a diagnosed GI condition, Microbiome Recovery or our GIT Protocol may be more appropriate. Contact us for a personalized recommendation.

How long until I see results? +

Many customers report noticeable improvements in digestion and energy within 2–4 weeks. Deeper microbiome restoration typically takes 2–3 months of consistent use.

Does Tundrex require refrigeration? +

No! Bacillus subtilis is a spore-forming probiotic, meaning it is highly stable at room temperature. No refrigeration needed — perfect for travel and everyday life.
