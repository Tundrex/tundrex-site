How to Follow the Microbiome Recovery Protocol

Total duration: 20 days

Phase 1 — Days 1–10

Tundrex 1.1

Take 10 capsules of Tundrex 1.1 per day for 10 days. For best results, take two capsules approximately every 4 hours — for example: at 7 am, 11 am, 3 pm, 7 pm, 11 pm — or choose your own consistent schedule. This phase delivers Bacillus subtilis to the small intestine to begin microbiome restoration.

Phase 2 — Days 11–20

Tundrex 3

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is the Microbiome Recovery protocol best for?+
This protocol is designed for anyone whose microbiome has been disrupted — including people recovering from antibiotic use, illness, or prolonged stress. It is ideal for those who want more than basic immune support and are ready for a more intensive 20-day regimen targeting both the small and large intestine.
Why are two boxes of Tundrex 1.1 included?+
Phase 1 of this protocol requires a higher dosage — 10 capsules per day (vs. 5 in the Immunity Boost) for 10 days, using 100 capsules total. This means 2 boxes of Tundrex 1.1 (50 capsules each) are needed for the first phase alone.
Can I take Tundrex 1.1 and Tundrex 3 at the same time?+
No — the protocol is designed as a sequential two-phase approach, not simultaneous. Complete the full 10-day Phase 1 (Tundrex 1.1) before beginning Phase 2 (Tundrex 3). This sequential approach allows each product to work optimally in its targeted intestinal environment.
How often should I repeat the Microbiome Recovery protocol?+
The Microbiome Recovery protocol is recommended to be repeated every 20–24 weeks (approximately 5–6 months). As a bonus, you will also maintain maximum immune support during each course.
Do I need to see a doctor before starting?+
Tundrex products are food supplements, not medications, and have no known side effects. However, we always recommend consulting your healthcare provider before starting any new supplement regimen — particularly if you have ongoing health conditions or are currently under medical care.
