How to Follow the Return to Well-Being Protocol

Total duration: 35 days across 3 phases

Phase 1 — Days 1–15

Tundrex 1.1

Take 10 capsules of Tundrex 1.1 per day for 15 days. For best results, take two capsules approximately every 4 hours — for example: at 7 am, 11 am, 3 pm, 7 pm, 11 pm — or choose your own consistent schedule. This intensive first phase delivers Bacillus subtilis to the small intestine to initiate deep microbiome restoration.

Phase 2 — Days 16–25

Tundrex 3

Once the Tundrex 1.1 phase is complete, take 5 capsules of Tundrex 3 per day for 10 days. Try taking one capsule every four hours. This phase extends microbiome restoration to the large intestine with Bacillus amyloliquefaciens.

Phase 3 — Days 26–35

Tundrex 4

Repeat: This full 35-day protocol can be repeated every 12–24 weeks as needed. Each course also provides maximum immune support as a bonus. Continuous intake throughout the day is highly advisable but not required.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is the Return to Well-Being protocol best for?+
This protocol is designed for individuals who want comprehensive, full-spectrum microbiome restoration. It is ideal for those who have experienced significant gut disruption, are dealing with ongoing digestive issues, or want the most thorough Tundrex regimen available. It covers both the small and large intestine across three sequential phases over 35 days.
What is the difference between Return to Well-Being and Microbiome Recovery?+
The Microbiome Recovery protocol is a 20-day, 2-product protocol (Tundrex 1.1 + Tundrex 3). Return to Well-Being is a more intensive 35-day, 3-product protocol (Tundrex 1.1 + Tundrex 3 + Tundrex 4) that adds a final phase targeting the small intestine again with Bacillus amyloliquefaciens (Tundrex 4). Return to Well-Being is the more comprehensive option.
Can I take the products simultaneously?+
No — the protocol is designed to be sequential, not simultaneous. Complete Phase 1 (Tundrex 1.1, days 1–15), then Phase 2 (Tundrex 3, days 16–25), then Phase 3 (Tundrex 4, days 26–35). This sequential approach allows each product to work in its targeted intestinal environment without interference.
How often should I repeat this protocol?+
The Return to Well-Being protocol can be repeated every 12–24 weeks (approximately 3–6 months) as needed. The appropriate frequency depends on your individual health goals and microbiome status. Consult your healthcare provider for personalized guidance.
Is this a treatment for any disease?+
No. All Tundrex products are food supplements, not medications or treatments. They are not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease.
