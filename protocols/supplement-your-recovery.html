How to Follow the Supplement Your Recovery Protocol

Total duration: 25 days across 2 phases

Phase 1 — Days 1–15

Tundrex 1.1

Take 10 capsules of Tundrex 1.1 per day for 15 days. For best results, take 2 capsules approximately every 4 hours — for example at 7 am, 11 am, 3 pm, 7 pm, and 11 pm — or set your own consistent schedule. This intensive phase delivers Bacillus subtilis deep into the small intestine to drive microbiome restoration.

Phase 2 — Days 16–25

Tundrex 3

Once the Tundrex 1.1 phase is complete, take 5 capsules of Tundrex 3 per day for 10 days. Take one capsule every four hours. This phase extends restoration to the large intestine with Bacillus amyloliquefaciens, completing comprehensive coverage of the digestive tract.

Repeat: This 25-day protocol can be repeated every 12–16 weeks as needed for sustained gut support. Continuous intake throughout the day is highly advisable but not required.

Note: Do not take Tundrex while on antibiotics — wait until your antibiotic course is complete before beginning. Tundrex is a food supplement, not a medication, and has no known side effects.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is the Supplement Your Recovery protocol best for?+
This protocol is ideal for people who need more intensive support than the Microbiome Recovery protocol but want to focus on the small and large intestine without a full 3-product regimen. It's particularly suited for those recovering from illness, antibiotic treatment, or experiencing persistent digestive disruption. The 3× Tundrex 1.1 phase provides a stronger small intestine intervention before moving to large intestine restoration with Tundrex 3.
How does this differ from the Microbiome Recovery protocol?+
The Microbiome Recovery protocol uses 2× Tundrex 1.1 over 10 days (10 capsules/day), then 1× Tundrex 3 over 10 days — totalling 20 days. Supplement Your Recovery uses 3× Tundrex 1.1 over 15 days (10 capsules/day), then 1× Tundrex 3 over 10 days — totalling 25 days. The key difference is the more intensive first phase: 50% more Tundrex 1.1 for a deeper small intestine intervention.
Can I take both phases simultaneously?+
No — the protocol is designed to be sequential, not simultaneous. Complete Phase 1 (Tundrex 1.1, days 1–15) fully before beginning Phase 2 (Tundrex 3, days 16–25). This sequential approach allows each product to work optimally in its targeted intestinal environment.
Should I upgrade to the Return to Well-Being protocol instead?+
The Return to Well-Being protocol adds a third phase with Tundrex 4 (another 10 days targeting the small intestine with Bacillus amyloliquefaciens). If you want the most comprehensive full-spectrum digestive tract restoration, Return to Well-Being is the right choice. Supplement Your Recovery is the right step up from Microbiome Recovery when you need a longer Phase 1 but aren't ready for the full 35-day commitment.
Does this protocol treat any disease?+
No. All Tundrex products are food supplements, not medications or treatments. They are not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease.
