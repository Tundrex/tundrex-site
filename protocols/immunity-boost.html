Frequently Asked Questions

Who is the Immunity Boost protocol for?+
The Immunity Boost is ideal for anyone looking to support their daily immune function and maintain a healthy gut microbiome. It is the recommended starting point for new Tundrex users — a simple, single-product 10-day course that can be repeated every 3–4 months.

Immunity Boost includes only 1 box of Tundrex 1.1 — a 10-day course. The Microbiome Recovery protocol adds Tundrex 3 for a more intensive 20-day regimen. The Return to Well-Being protocol uses all three Tundrex products over 35 days for comprehensive full-system restoration. Immunity Boost is the entry-level option for ongoing maintenance.

It is recommended to repeat the Immunity Boost protocol every 3–4 months to maintain maximum immune and microbiome support. One person needs 1 box of Tundrex 1.1 per course.

For deeper microbiome support, we recommend upgrading to the Microbiome Recovery or Return to Well-Being protocols rather than simply taking extra boxes of Tundrex 1.1 alone. Those protocols add Tundrex 3 and/or Tundrex 4 for broader digestive tract coverage.
