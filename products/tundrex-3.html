Frequently Asked Questions

What is Tundrex 3?+
Tundrex 3 is a probiotic food supplement containing Bacillus amyloliquefaciens — a spore-forming strain sourced from pristine organic Siberian tundra soil. It is specifically active in the large intestine, where it helps support microbiome normalization and immune defenses.
Can I take Tundrex 3 by itself?+
Tundrex 3 is most effective when used as part of a multi-phase protocol, following a Tundrex 1.1 course. Tundrex 1.1 lays the groundwork in the small intestine, and Tundrex 3 extends that support into the large intestine. Using them in sequence as part of the Microbiome Recovery or Return to Well-Being protocols provides the best results.
What is Bacillus amyloliquefaciens?+
Bacillus amyloliquefaciens is a naturally occurring, spore-forming bacteria closely related to Bacillus subtilis. It is found in soil and plant environments and has been studied for its potential probiotic properties, including supporting gut microbiome diversity and immune function. Like other Bacillus strains, it forms spores that are highly resistant to heat and stomach acid, ensuring delivery to the lower digestive tract.
How does Tundrex 3 differ from Tundrex 1.1 and Tundrex 4?+
Tundrex 1.1 contains Bacillus subtilis and is active in the small intestine. Tundrex 3 contains Bacillus amyloliquefaciens and is active in the large intestine. Tundrex 4 also contains Bacillus amyloliquefaciens but is active in the small intestine. Together, the three products provide comprehensive coverage across the full digestive tract.
Does Tundrex 3 need to be refrigerated?+
No. Like all Tundrex products, Tundrex 3 is a spore-forming probiotic that remains stable at room temperature for up to 2 years. No refrigeration is required.
Are there side effects?+
As a natural probiotic supplement, Tundrex 3 has no known side effects and no reported cases of overdose. It contains small amounts of corn, potato, and sugar. Always consult your healthcare provider if you have specific health conditions or concerns before starting any new supplement.
